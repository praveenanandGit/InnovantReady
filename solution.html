    OUR PROJECTS AND SOLUTIONS

    Rooftop Solar

    We at Innovant Energotech deliver lean and innovative solutions to harness solar energy to power your daily energy needs ranging from lighting to air-conditioning and refrigeration. Depending upon the space available, solar rooftop solutions are sufficient to power homes, shops, dispensaries, small offices, and big commercial and industrial units.

    Commercial

    At Innovant Energotech we understand the needs of energy intensive large industrial and commercial units. Withour solar solutions, industrial and commercial units not only get more reliable electricity supply than grid supply and diesel generators but also at more affordable rates. With the option of net-metering the entities can sell back the extra electricity generated at their end to the government and thus becoming a Prosumer than just being a consumer.

    Rural Solutions

    Understanding the special needs of rural communities we not only provide solar rooftop solutions but as well as irrigation solutions through solar sensor pumps and submersibles. Solar sensor pumps or solar submersibles couples with other irrigation technologies can irrigate your crops without the need for grid power or expensive diesel generators.

    Special Projects

    At Innovant Energotech we also design and develop large scale solar projects ranging upto several megawatts. We also undertake and provide Smart Energy System Management Services.
